About ACT

American College of Technology (ACT) provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and online learning programs designed to prepare students for industry and professional careers.

Academic Programs

Undergraduate Programs

  • BSc in Computer Science
  • BA in Business Administration

Postgraduate Programs

  • MSc in Computer Science
  • MBA (Master of Business Administration)

Online Learning

ACT Academy is an online learning platform where students can access courses in programming, business, design, and more at their own pace.

Student Communities

ACT supports student clubs and communities such as the ACT DEV Community, where students collaborate, learn, and grow their technical skills.
