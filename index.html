Welcome to American College of Technology

American College of Technology (ACT) is a higher learning institution dedicated to developing globally competitive professionals in Business Administration and Computer Science.

ACT offers flexible learning through on-campus and online programs, making education accessible for busy students and working professionals.

Why Choose ACT?

  • Fully accredited by the Ethiopian Higher Education Agency
  • Flexible evening, weekend, and online classes
  • Globally competitive curriculum
  • Exclusive Coursera partnership in Ethiopia
  • Affordable and industry-focused programs

Our Programs

Undergraduate Programs

  • BSc in Computer Science
  • BA in Business Administration

Postgraduate & Online Programs

  • MSc in Computer Science
  • MBA (On-campus & Online)
  • MA in Project Management

ACT Academy

ACT Academy is an online learning platform that offers flexible, professional training programs in Digital Marketing, Data Science, Programming, and more.

Learners can study at their own pace and access both free and paid courses anytime, anywhere.

ACT DEV Community

The ACT DEV Community is a student-led technology club that brings together developers and tech enthusiasts to collaborate, learn, and improve their practical skills.
