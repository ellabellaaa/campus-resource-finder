What is ACT Academy?

ACT Academy is our professional training division offering short-term, practical courses for skill development and career advancement.

Different from our degree programs: Academy courses are shorter (1-3 months), focused on specific skills, and designed for working professionals and students seeking additional qualifications.

Who Should Join ACT Academy?

Working Professionals

Upgrade your skills while working full-time with our flexible evening and weekend schedules.

University Students

Gain practical skills beyond your degree to enhance employability.

Career Changers

Learn new skills to transition into high-demand fields like tech and digital marketing.

Entrepreneurs

Acquire business and technical skills to start or grow your business.
