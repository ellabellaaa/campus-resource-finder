ACT offers degree programs designed to prepare you for global career opportunities. All programs include practical projects and industry-relevant curriculum.

Undergraduate Degree Programs

4-Year Bachelor's Degrees

BSc in Computer Science

Comprehensive computer science education covering programming, software engineering, databases, networks, and security.

Core Courses:

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Systems
  • Web Development
  • Network Security
  • Software Engineering

Career Paths:

Software Developer, Web Developer, Database Administrator, Network Engineer, IT Consultant

BA in Business Administration

Business management education covering accounting, marketing, finance, human resources, and entrepreneurship.

Core Courses:

  • Principles of Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Law
  • Strategic Management
  • Entrepreneurship

Career Paths:

Business Manager, Marketing Specialist, Financial Analyst, HR Manager, Entrepreneur

Postgraduate Degree Programs

2-Year Master's Degrees

MSc in Computer Science

Advanced study in computer science with research components and specialization options.

Specializations:

  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence

Admission Requirement: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

MBA Program

Program Features:

  • Case study methodology
  • Industry projects
  • Leadership development

Admission Requirement: Bachelor's degree in any field + work experience preferred
