Who We Are

American College of Technology (ACT) is a higher learning institution committed to developing globally competitive professionals in the fields of Business Administration and Computer Science.

ACT delivers its programs through flexible learning systems that include on-campus, online, evening, and weekend classes.

Our Learning Approach

  • Virtual and on-campus learning options
  • Evening and weekend class schedules
  • Block course system for flexibility
  • Paperless learning using modern LMS
  • Free soft skills training for students

Accreditation & Recognition

ACT is fully accredited by the Ethiopian Higher Education Agency and works to maintain international education standards.

Our faculty members include experienced local and international educators from reputable academic institutions.

Our Vision

To become a leading institution of higher learning in the region by delivering quality, flexible, and affordable education that meets global standards.
