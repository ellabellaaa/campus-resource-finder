Our History

Founded in 2015, ACT began with a vision to provide quality higher education in Ethiopia with international standards.

We started with two programs and have grown to become a leading institution in Business and Computer Science education.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop globally competitive professionals through quality, flexible, and affordable education in Business Administration and Computer Science.

Our Vision

To be the leading higher education institution in the region recognized for excellence in professional education.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to high academic standards
  • Innovation: Embracing modern teaching methods
  • Integrity: Ethical practices in all operations
  • Student Focus: Putting student success first
  • Global Perspective: Preparing students for international opportunities

Accreditation & Recognition

ACT is fully accredited by the Ethiopian Higher Education Agency (EHEA).

Our programs meet national and international quality standards.

Partnerships:

  • Official Coursera Campus Partner in Ethiopia
  • Collaborations with international universities
  • Industry partnerships for practical training

Our Leadership

ACT is led by experienced educators and industry professionals committed to educational excellence.

Our faculty includes PhD holders, industry experts, and international lecturers.
